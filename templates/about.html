Why is Water Important?

Water is essential for all living beings. It plays a vital role in human health, agriculture, and ecosystems. Clean water is needed for drinking, cooking, and sanitation.

Why is Water Quality Important?

Water quality determines whether it is safe for consumption and use. Contaminated water can cause diseases, harm the environment, and reduce agricultural yield.

How to Know Water Quality Parameters?

Water quality can be measured through various parameters such as pH level, hardness, turbidity, and chemical composition. Testing water regularly helps ensure its safety.
